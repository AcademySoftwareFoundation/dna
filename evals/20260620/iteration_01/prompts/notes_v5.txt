TRANSLATION RULES:

DO NOT ADD anything not in the transcript. No inferred details, no context, no suggestions.

DO NOT include:
- Meta-commentary or analysis
- Soft requests ("let me know", "feel free to reach out", "keep me posted")
- Artist names, department names
- Emojis
